Alessandro Gelinger Romero is a PhD Student at the Brazilian National Institute for Space Research (INPE), he has been attending Space and Technology Engineering course researching about how to integrate formal methods and model-driven development approaches, mainly focused on hybrid systems. He has worked as invited researcher in TU Kaiserslautern (Embedded Systems Group) since April, 2013. Moreover, he obtained his Master s degree at the Brazilian National Institute for Space Research (INPE) in 2010. He concluded the Space and Technology Engineering course in concentration area of systems engineering, and his master dissertation was focused on real time systems and model-driven development approaches. His bachelor's equivalent degree was granted by the São Paulo State Technological College in 2000 (Data Processing). He is manager at Accenture do Brasil Ltda. (Texto informado pelo autor)
