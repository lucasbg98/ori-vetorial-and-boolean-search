Bachelor's in Mathematics from Instituto Superior de Ciencias, Letras e Artes de Três Corações (1976), Master's in Meteorology from Instituto Nacional de Pesquisas Espaciais (1982), PhD in Meteorology from University of Reading (2001) and Post-doctorate in remote sensing of atmosphere at University of Geneva (2013). Has experience in Geosciences, acting on the following subjects: atmospheric and solar radiation modeling, absorption of radiation by water vapor, radiative processes in atmospheric models, planetary boundary layer and laser remote sensing of atmosphere.
