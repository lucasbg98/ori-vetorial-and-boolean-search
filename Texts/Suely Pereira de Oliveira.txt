Suely Pereira de Oliveira has been a Full Professor at the University of Iowa since July 2008. She received her undergraduate degree at Universidade Federal de Pernambuco (1984), master's and PhD in Applied Mathematics University of Colorado at Denver (1993). She was a researcher at the Australian National Universtiy (1993-1994) and was Assistant Professor at Texas AM University (1994-1998). Her research areas include various topics in Numerical Analysis, Scientific and Parallel Computing, Clustering Algorithms, Applications in Engineering and Bioinformatics. She was a visting researcher at Universidade Federal de Sao Paulo (2009-2010) and is co-author of the book Writing Scientific Software (Cambridge University, 2006). (Texto informado pelo autor)
