Ronaldo Martins da Ponte received the B.Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte, in 2007. From 2008 through 2011 he worked at Chemtech a Siemens Company, developing projects for Oil and Gas industry. His main research interests are related to bioinspired electronics (neuromorphic circuits), RF electronics, ultra-low power electronics, ultra-low voltage electronics and microelectronics design.
