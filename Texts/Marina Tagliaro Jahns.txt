Pharmacist specialized in Medical Analysis, has a Master's degree in Cellular and Molecular Biology (2008 UFRGS Brazil), a Master's degree in Genomics and Plant Productivity (2010 Paris Diderot University France) and a PhD in Biology (2014 Paris Sud University France). Has experience in molecular genetics and homologous recombination, as well as neddylation/rubylation. Works with several different genetic and cytological techniques, including classical cytogenetic analysis of meiotic cells, protein inmunolocalisation, in situ hybridization, light and epifluorescence microscopy, recombination analysis, cloning of DNA fragments, production of competent E.coli/A. tumefaciens, RNA interference, in vitro plant culture, Agroinfiltration/transformation of Arabidopsis plants.
