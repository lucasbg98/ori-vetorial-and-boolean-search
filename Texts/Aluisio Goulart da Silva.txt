I majored in Animal Science in 1997 at Federal University of Viçosa. At that same year, I began my Master course in Rural Economics at the same school. I work at Embrapa Rice and Beans as Analyst of the sector of Demand Prospecting since 2006. This position is linked to the Technology Transfer Sector. I have experience with intelectual property issues. I have coordinated a project focused on geographical indication. Other areas of interest are: rural communication, total quality management, agribussiness and integrated production systems. Since Feb. 2012 I've being developing my Research Doctorate in Agricultural, Environmental and Food Science and Technology at University of Bologna, Italy. The focus of my research is food quality assurance schemes, especifically on Integrated Production System.
