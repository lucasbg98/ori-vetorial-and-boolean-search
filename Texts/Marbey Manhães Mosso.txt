Graduated in electrical engineering (1972) and master degree in electrical engineering (1975) at Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-RJ). At this moment, is the head of microwaves and optical systems group in the Telecommunications center at this university (CETUC) coordinating RD contracts with Brazilian agencies and private companies. Since 1975 has been involved with microwave components, GAAS circuits and devices, printed antennas, optical components and networks. At this moment is developing projects in Gigabit Ethernet, FPGA and RFID. PHD equivalence approved at PUC-RJ in October/2008. (Texto informado pelo autor)
