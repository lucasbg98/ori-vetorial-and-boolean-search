Bachelor's at Electrical Engineering from Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul (2004), master's at Electrical Engineering from Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul (2006) and PhD at Electrical Engineering from Paulista State University Júlio de Mesquita Filho (2010). Currently, he is Professor at Electrical Department in Federal University of Mato Grosso do Sul. He has experience in Electrical Engineering, focusing on Magnetic Circuits, Power Electronics and Power Quality, acting on the following subjects: modeling, simulation, harmonic power flow, dc ac converter and distributed generation.
