Márjory has worked with a variety of subjects, always multidisciplinary though mostly related with artificial intelligence and biology. Her main topics of interest are biometrics (psychological and physiological aspects) and identity management, effects of ageing, data mining for forensics scenario, social networks security, bioinformatics and medical image processing, machine learning techniques (multiclassifier systems) and multiagent systems. Currently, she works as lecturer in artificial intelligence of the DIMAp/CCET/UFRN Natal/RN/Brazil. She is also part of the Image and Information Processing group of the School of Engineering and Digital Arts of the University of Kent at Canterbury UK. (Texto informado pelo autor)
