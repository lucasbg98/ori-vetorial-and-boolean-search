Francini Osses Martins is a Registered Professional Biologist in British Columbia, Canada with over eight years of experience in Brazil and Canada. Ms. Martins currently works with Contaminated Sites (investigation, remediation and monitoring programs), and her broad experience includes parental investiment, site selection, entomology, ethogram, lab analysis and ecotox tests, excavation of contaminated soil and water, groundwater and soil vapour monitoring, soil treatment at land treatment facilities, ISO and CALA accreditation. Ms.Martins has published in peer reviewed journals and has worked on proposals, budgets and wrote several Phase I, II and III reports, including PSI, DSI and COR. (Texto informado pelo autor)
