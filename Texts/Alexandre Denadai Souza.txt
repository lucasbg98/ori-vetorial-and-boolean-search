PhD in Sciences by the Department of Pharmacology at Institute of Biomedical Sciences University of São Paulo (USP) and a Post-doctoral training at the National Institute of Pharmacology and Molecular Biology at Federal University of São Paulo (Unifesp), in addition to previous international experiences during a Sandwich PhD and a BEPE Post-doctoral training at Inserm (Toulouse, France), nowadays is a Post-doctoral Researcher funded by the European Research Council (ERC). Has experience in molecular and cellular biology of immune responses, mainly in the investigation of vascular, cellular and molecular mechanisms of autoimmune diseases, inflammation and neurobiology of pain.
