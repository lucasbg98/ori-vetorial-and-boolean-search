Graduated in Biology at the University of Campinas (2001), where she also got her master´s (2004) and PhD (2008) degree in Ecology. Part of her PhD was done at the University of California (Davis), at the Anthropology department (Evolutionary Wing). She is also a co-founder of the non-profit Fisheries and Food Institute. Her main research lines are small-scale fisheries, fishers' behavior and strategies and fisheries co-management. (Texto informado pelo autor)
