Graduated in geology from the Universidade Estadual Paulista Julio de Mesquita Filho (1983). He is currently a partner owner System Mud Industria e Comercio Ltda. He has experience in Geosciences with an emphasis in Hydrogeology, Drilling and Drilling Fluids, acting on the following topics: clays, rheology, polymer additives, rehabilitation, bentonite, construction of wells and boreholes for mineral exploration. It operates since 2000 in development of products for drilling fluids, completion and maintenance of wells. (Texto informado pelo autor)
