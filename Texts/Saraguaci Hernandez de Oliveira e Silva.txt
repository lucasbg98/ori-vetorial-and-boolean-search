Saraguaci Hernandez Oliveira e Silva, formed in the Faculty of Education from the Pontifical Catholic University (PUC) of Campinas in 1993. In December 1994, he entered the medical course (UNICAMP), especially as a student. In 2001, 2005, 2006, respectively, obtained a master's degree and doctorate (CAPES scholarship, CNPq), and Post Doctorate (FAPESP) in Pharmacology, in the neuroscience lab (SNP), the State University of Campinas School of Medical Sciences (FCM / UNICAMP). He currently serves as Professor collaborative scientific researcher in the Department of Pharmacology, Faculty of Medical Sciences FCM / UNICAMP, developing research on lines of research: Toxins from venomous animals in the neuromuscular junction, Depression in the elderly, Brain Injury consequent depression (Glioblastoma GBM) and Myasthenia Gravis. Has acted as the referee FAEPEX, in the judgment of scientific papers and release of funds for projects, the Faculty of Medical Sciences-FCM / UNICAMP. (Texto informado pelo autor)
