Graduaçao em Biologia e em Ecologia. Master em Gestao e Conservaçao da Biodiversidade. Mestrado e doutorado em Ecologia pela Universidade de Sevilha (homologados pela UFRGS). Tenho trabalhado com aves e colaborado em estudos com répteis, anfíbios e peixes. Minhas líneas de pesquisa sao ecologia aplicada à dinâmica populacional usando diversas ferramentas, impacto de estruturas antrópicas aplicada à dinâmica populacional de espécies afetadas e biologia da conservaçao. Dentro do estudo de estruturas antrópicas, metodologias de EIAs, estudos prévios durante e pós construçao objetivando metodologias de evitem e/ou minimizem o impacto ambiental. Degree in Biology in 1999 and a degree in Ecology in the year 2001. Coastal Aquatic Ecology Specialist (FURG- BRAZIL) and Quantum Statistics (UPO- SPAIN). Master in Biodiversity Conservation (SPAIN). PHD in Ecology (US- SPAIN). Postdoctoral research in the Laboratory of Zoology and Animal Genetics, Federal University of Pelotas (UFPel). I have been focusing in understanding the complexity of a population dynamics in order to address the conservation of the population/species. In my research, I combine Applied Biology and Ecology with Population Dynamics, using several tools such as Biochemistry, Genetics, Field Ecology and Environmental Impact Assessment of anthropogenic structures. This multidisciplinary approach is needed since species survival depends on both internal (e.g. breeding success) and external factors (e.g. environmental quality and resources). Previously, I have worked mainly with wetland birds, or related to water bodies, in Brazil and steppe birds, or related to grain crops, in Spain. "Temporary Assistant Professor" at the UFRGS during 2012. Actually working as Associate Professor at the UFERSA. (Texto informado pelo autor)
