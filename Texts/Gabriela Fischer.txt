Gabriela Fischer received her specialization s degree in Exercise Physiology (2006) and her master s degree in Human Movement Sciences (2010) from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul, Porto Alegre (Brazil). In May 2013, she received her Ph.D. degree in Science of Physical Exercise and Human Movement at the University of Verona (Italy). Gabriela s principal area of research interest is bioenergetics and biomechanics of human locomotion and Paralympic sports. (Texto informado pelo autor)
