Graduated in Biological Sciences from the State University of Paraíba (1989), MA in cryptogams (area of concentration phycology) Federal University of Pernambuco (1996) and a Ph.D. in Ecology and Natural Resources, Federal University of São Carlos (2002). Currently he is professor at the State University of Paraíba. Has experience in the area of ecology with emphasis on ecology of aquatic ecosystems in semi-arid tropics, acting principalmene the following themes: taxonomy and ecology of phytoplankton, eutrophication, algal periphyton and operation and ecological processes in reservoirs.
