Claudia is currently a Post-Doctoral fellow at Leslie L. Dan Faculty of Pharmacy at University of Toronto in Canada. She has a Ph.D. in Inorganic Chemistry, B.Sc. and B.Ed. degrees from the University of Sao Paulo in Brazil. During almost eight years of experience in research she gained valuable experience in synthesis and characterization of inorganic nanomaterials and their association with drug molecules as well as formulations and drug delivery systems, going from fundamental characterization to in vivo essays.
