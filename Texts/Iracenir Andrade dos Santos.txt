Bachelor s degree in Agronomic Engineering at the Federal Rural University of Amazon (2003), master s degree in Entomology at the Federal University of Viçosa, with a focus on theoretical ecology and PhD at the Federal University of Viçosa with a focus on applied ecology. Dr. Dos Santos was Visiting Scholar at the University of Michigan (from 10/2009 to 10/2010, Sandwich Program of CNPq). Postdoctoral at the Federal University of Alfenas CAPES/PNPD. Currently Dr. Dos Santos is Professor/Researcher et the Federal University of Western Pará and coordinator of the PET/Program. (Texto informado pelo autor)
