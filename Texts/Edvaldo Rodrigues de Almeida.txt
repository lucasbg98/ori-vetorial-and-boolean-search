Graduate in Biomedicine at the Federal University of Pernambuco (1977), he devoted himself to research and education. He made his Masters in psychobiology by Federal University of São Paulo Brazil-EPM (1995) and PhD in Bioactive Natural Products and Synthetic at the Federal University of Paraíba (LTF) Brazil. He is currently Associate Professor in the Department of Antibiotics, Federal University of Pernambuco, Brazil. (Texto informado pelo autor)
