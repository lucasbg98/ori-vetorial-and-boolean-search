Veterinarian (Federal University of Uberlândia 1999), expertise in beef cattle (University of Northern Paraná 2008). Master of Veterinarian Sciences (Federal University of Uberlândia 2010) and PhD of Animal Science (Federal University of Goiás 2013). Assistant professor of veterinary medicine graduation (UNIPAC- Uberlândia- 2007- 2013). Postdoctoral fellowship (CNPq) underway by Southern Illinois University Carbondale, USA, under the guidance of Professor Dr. Eduardo Leite Gastal. Experience in animal reproduction, acting on following subjects: embryo transfer, transvaginal follicular aspiration, in vitro cell culture, ultrassonografy, andrology and preantral follicles manipulation.
