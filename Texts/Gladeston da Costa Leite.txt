Gladeston da Costa Leite was born in Fortaleza, CE, Brazil in 1972, received the B.Sc. degree in mathematics from the Federal University of Ceara (UFC) in 1998, the M.Sc. degree in mathematics from UFC, in 2001 and the Ph.D degree class in Teleinformatics Engineering from the Federal University of Ceara (UFC) in 2011. In 2003, he joined State University of Ceara (UECE) in the Mathematics Department. His main interests include SAR image processing and analysis for remote sensing of environment, shape analysis and computer vision. M.Sc. Leite is currently a Full Professor in Mathematics Department, at State University of Ceara, Brazil. Her publishing activities include papers in International Journal Sensors, the leading international, peer-reviewed open access journal on the science and technology of sensors and biosensors. Sensors is published by MDPI online monthly. (Texto informado pelo autor)
